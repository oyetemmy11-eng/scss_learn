Bitcoin

Bitcoin is a decentralised digital currency that operates without a central authority or bank. It's the first and most well-known cryptocurrency, created by an anonymous person or group known as Satoshi Nakamoto in 2009

Ethereum

Ethereum is decentralized, open-source blockchain platform that allows developers to build and deploy decentralised applications (dApps). Think of it as global computer that anyone can accessand use to create applications that operates without a central authority.

Blockchain

A blockchain is a decentralized, distributed digital ledger that securely records transactions in blocks that are chronologically chained together. Because it is shared across a network of computers, it is transparent and resistant to tampering, with changes requiring consensus from the network.
